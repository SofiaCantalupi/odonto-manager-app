# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
A comprehensive dental clinic management application focused on streamlining patient records, odontograms, and budgeting while managing procedures for better profitability and simplifying billing.

## Target Audience
- **Dentists/Clinicians:** Primary users for clinical and administrative tasks, including odontogram management and treatment planning.
- **Admin Staff:** Users responsible for scheduling, patient onboarding, and front-office financial management.

## Core Goals
- **Clinic Management:** Streamline patient records, odontograms, and budgeting.
- **Treatment Focus:** Manage procedures and pricing for better profitability.
- **Financial Manager:** Simplify billing and payment plans for patients.
- **Scheduling:** Manage the agenda and patient shifts effectively.

## Key Features
- **Interactive Odontogram:** Graphical representation of dental health with real-time synchronization.
- **Budget Management:** Flexible financing options (fixed-quotes and open-balance) and quote-based payment scheduling.
- **Patient Records:** Complete profile and history tracking, including personal and insurance information.
- **Agenda & Scheduling:** Manage patient appointments, shifts, and the clinic's daily calendar.

## Success Metrics
- **Admin Efficiency:** Reduction in time spent on administrative paperwork.
- **Data Integrity:** Ensuring all clinical records are complete and accurate.

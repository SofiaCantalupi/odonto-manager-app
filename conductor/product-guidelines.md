# Product Guidelines

## UX Principles
- **Speed & Clarity:** Focus on high-speed data entry and clear visual feedback for busy clinical environments.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure the interface is accessible for all staff members, including those with different needs.
- **Responsiveness:** Provide a smooth, responsive experience on all devices, from desktop stations to tablets.

## Prose Style
- **Professional & Concise:** Use clear, direct, and efficient language suitable for busy professionals. Avoid unnecessary jargon or fluff.

## Branding & Tone
- **Modern & Tech-Forward:** Employ cutting-edge visuals and a sleek design to create a state-of-the-art clinic feel. Focus on clean lines and interactive elements.

## Accessibility Standards
- **Core Accessibility Features:** Target high-impact accessibility features, including full keyboard navigation support, screen reader compatibility for critical workflows, and clear focus states.

## Design Guidelines
- Use **Tailwind CSS v4** for utility-first styling.
- Employ **Lucide Icons** for consistent, modern iconography.
- Maintain a **High-Contrast** color palette for readability in clinical settings.
- Use **OnPush Change Detection** and **Angular Signals** for a performant, reactive UI.
